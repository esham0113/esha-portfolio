Passions

Kathak

I have been doing an Indian classical dance called Kathak for 10 years. Kathak originated within Hindu temples as a storytelling device for portraying tales from Hindu scriptures. I had my Visharad (graduation) in November 2023, performing 6 dances in front of 500 guests, which takes over a year to prepare for.

I have also taught Kathak to younger students and enjoy performing in recitals, weddings, and other events.

Helping Beez

I founded the nonprofit organization Helping Beez to give back to the community and provide awareness to future generations about the issues our planet is facing. Past projects about water filtration systems, city accessibility, and space inspired me to start this initiative.

Our mission includes:

  • Planting trees
  • Providing education to underprivileged children
  • Providing safe homes for animals
  • Bringing water to communities in need using in-ground water pumps
